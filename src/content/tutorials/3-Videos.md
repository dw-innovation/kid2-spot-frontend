## Prompting on SPOT
A good prompt in SPOT is a clear sentence. Simply describe what you see and SPOT will find matching places on OpenStreetMap. Turn up the volume and master prompting.

[youtube](https://youtube.com/embed/wfLSdDrWMC4,/images/posters/introduction.avif)

## Viewing Results on SPOT
