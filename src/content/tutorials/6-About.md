Spot was created by DW innovation and is co-funded by the German Secretary of State for Culture and the Media (BKM) via the KID2 Project (Künstliche Intelligenz gegen Desinformation #2)
Spot is an AI-powered tool that aims to comply to the principles of Trustworthy AI. You can read more about these principles and how we have implemented them in Spot in the “Trusted AI Principles” section.
